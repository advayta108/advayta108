# Code of Conduct

We strive to create a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone who contributes to `advayta108/advayta108`.

## Our Standards

All contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional when interacting with others.
- Assume good intent and address issues constructively.
- Provide feedback that is helpful, specific, and actionable.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes (but is not limited to):

- Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language.
- Disruptive or threatening behavior.
- Unwanted or unsolicited attention of a sexual or discriminatory nature.
- Retaliation against someone who reported unacceptable behavior.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, report it privately via GitHub Discussions or by contacting the maintainers listed on the repository profile.

## Security

For security-related issues, please follow our security policy in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).

## Enforcement

The maintainers may remove contributions or take other action deemed appropriate to maintain a safe community environment.
